01 / SYSTEM

Not a chatbot.
A mission control.

Linear chat hides the work. DAClaw runs a transparent loop — connect the ground truth, compile and steer the plan, then execute and deliver. Every step is inspectable, and nothing heavy runs until you approve it.

STEP 01
⌗

Connect & Ground

Attach tables, warehouses (Postgres, MySQL, Snowflake, DuckDB…), and a local knowledge corpus of PDF, DOCX, Markdown, JSON, and HTML. Every answer is grounded in read-only SQL and cited evidence.

Live databasesLocal filesKnowledge fusion
STEP 02
⌘

Compile & Steer

One question compiles into a typed workflow DAG. DAClaw validates dependencies, cycles, and execution layers, opens a node inspector on every step, asks clarifying questions when it must — and waits for your Approve & Run.

Typed DAGNode inspectorApprove gate
STEP 03
◫

Execute & Deliver

Independent branches run in parallel, then converge into dashboards, reports, native Excel, dbt pipelines, and narrated data videos. Solved a hard task? The agent saves a quality-validated skill for next time.

05 / RESEARCH PLATFORM

A workbench that
measures itself.

DAClaw is also a full-stack data-agent research platform. The same product surfaces feed an evaluation cockpit that scores every model × harness across the entire data-agent lifecycle — and routes what it learns back into the workflows, tools, and playbooks the agent uses.

  • Model × Harness × Benchmark matrix — one grid, the whole field.
  • Full-stack taxonomy — SQL · Analysis · Data Engineering · Spreadsheet · Visualization.
  • Trajectory & artifact diagnostics — grade the path, not just the answer.
  • Evaluation protocol — Completion · Validity · Editability, calibrated to reported SOTA.
  • Closed loop — evaluation feeds back into workflow, tools, and playbooks.
